Introduction

We have decided to develop a web app to help students to rely on mental health professionals by sharing some videos, tips, and testimonials of ¡Supérate! Ambassadors through Other-self web app, students will be able to get the necessary information to understand different psychological terms, clear up myths and know the proper steps to seek professional help

Our goals


  • Objective

  • Promote students to have the confidence in the psychological sections.

  • Mision

  • Help students of ¡Supérate! Fundación Poma to generate confidence by receiveing psychological help.

  • Vision

  • Provide the necessary tools to have an understanding of the psychological sections.
